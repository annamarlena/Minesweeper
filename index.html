TrooperSweeper
TrooperSweeper
DIFFICULT
In a galaxy far, far away, your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to mark an active Strormtrooper as quickly as possible, without allowing him to shoot you. (But don’t worry, they have really bad aim.) Begin by clicking any grid. If the grid you marked does not contain a Stormtrooper, it will either display how many Stormtroopers currently surround it or display a blank if none are detected nearby. If you suspect a grid contains a Stormtrooper, click the Stormtrooper image BEFORE clicking the grid in question.This marks the grid as dangerous.The game is over when every grid has either been cleared or correctly marked with a Stormtrooper. May the Force be with you!GOOD LUCK!
